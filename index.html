The Pattern

AfterBoards' moat is student lifecycle tools — eligibility, cutoffs, loans, financing, forums, gamification. These are engagement layers around mock tests, not core learning infrastructure. Preparoo's moat is content volume and learning depth — NCERT integration, chapter-wise practice, spaced repetition, and AI-powered study. As exam prep shifts toward holistic learning platforms, Preparoo's bet is structurally stronger.
